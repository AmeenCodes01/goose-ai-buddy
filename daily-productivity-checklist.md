## ðŸ’ª Work Focus Techniques

### Getting Started
- [ ] **Start with hardest task** when energy is highest (Eat the Frog)
- [ ] **Use Pomodoro Technique** - 25 min work, 5 min break cycles
- [ ] **Single-tasking** - focus on one task at a time
- [ ] **Phone in airplane mode** or another room during deep work

### During Work Sessions
- [ ] **Time-box tasks** - set specific time limits for activities
- [ ] **Use "Two-Minute Rule"** - if task takes <2 minutes, do it now
- [ ] **Batch similar tasks** together (emails, calls, admin work)
- [ ] **Track progress** - check off completed items for motivation

### Managing Distractions
- [ ] **Close unnecessary browser tabs** and applications
- [ ] **Use website blockers** for social media during work hours
- [ ] **Communicate boundaries** - let others know your focused work times
- [ ] **Keep notepad handy** for random thoughts that pop up

## â¸ï¸ Break Recommendations

### Micro-breaks (Every 25-30 minutes)
- [ ] **Stand and stretch** - combat sitting fatigue
- [ ] **Look away from screen** - follow 20-20-20 rule (20 seconds, 20 feet, every 20 minutes)
- [ ] **Deep breathing** - 3-5 conscious breaths
- [ ] **Drink water** - stay hydrated throughout the day

### Short Breaks (Every 1-2 hours)
- [ ] **Walk around** - get blood flowing (5-10 minutes)
- [ ] **Snack mindfully** - healthy options like nuts, fruit
- [ ] **Chat with colleague** or call a friend (social connection)
- [ ] **Step outside** - fresh air and natural light

### Lunch Break (30-60 minutes)
- [ ] **Eat away from desk** - proper meal break
- [ ] **Go for walk** or light exercise
- [ ] **Practice gratitude** - reflect on positive aspects of day
- [ ] **Avoid work-related content** - true mental break

### Afternoon Energy Boost
- [ ] **Power nap** if possible (10-20 minutes max)
- [ ] **Healthy snack** to combat afternoon dip
- [ ] **Switch tasks** - move to lighter work if energy is low
- [ ] **Get natural light** - sit by window or go outside briefly

- [ ] **Close all work applications** - clear mental boundary
- [ ] **Write in gratitude journal** - 3 things you're grateful for
- [ ] **Plan evening activities** - relaxation, family time, hobbies
- [ ] **Avoid screens 1 hour before bed** - better sleep quality
